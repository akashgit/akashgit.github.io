## Research & Alignment

- **[LAB: Large-Scale Alignment for ChatBots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.01081)** - Research paper introducing the LAB method powering InstructLab, enabling efficient large-scale alignment of language models. Senior author: Akash Srivastava. *arXiv, March 2024*

- **[Researchers from IBM and MIT Introduce LAB](https://www.marktechpost.com/2024/03/19/researchers-from-ibm-and-mit-introduce-lab-a-novel-ai-method-designed-to-overcome-the-scalability-challenges-in-the-instruction-tuning-phase-of-large-language-model-llm-training/)** - External press coverage analyzing the LAB method's approach to scalable instruction tuning and alignment. *MarkTechPost, March 2024*

- **[IBM Patents a Faster Method to Train LLMs](https://www.bigdatawire.com/2024/04/08/ibm-patents-a-faster-method-to-train-llms-for-enterprises/)** - Coverage of LAB method patent application for enterprise LLM training. *BigDataWire, April 2024*

- **[What is alignment in AI?](https://research.ibm.com/blog/what-is-alignment-ai)** - Comprehensive overview of LLM alignment techniques, challenges, and IBM Research's approach to building aligned AI systems. *IBM Research Blog*

- **[Synthetic training data for LLMs](https://research.ibm.com/blog/LLM-generated-data)** - Technical blog on synthetic data generation methods for model training, evaluation, and alignment. *IBM Research Blog*

- **[Curiosity-Driven Red-Teaming for Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.19464)** - Research on automated red-teaming approaches for evaluating robustness of agentic systems and language models. *arXiv, February 2024*

- **[Dr. SoW: Density Ratio Methods for Preference Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.14402)** - Novel preference learning approach reducing annotation costs while improving alignment quality. *arXiv, February 2024*
